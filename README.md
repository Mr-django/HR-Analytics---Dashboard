# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze employee attrition and identify the factors influencing workforce turnover. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of employee demographics, salary distribution, education, job roles, and years of service, enabling HR teams to make informed, data-driven decisions to improve employee retention.

---

## Objective

The objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze employee attrition trends.
- Identify departments and employee groups with high turnover.
- Understand the impact of age, salary, education, and experience on attrition.
- Support HR professionals in developing effective retention strategies.

## Key Insights

- The overall employee attrition rate is **16.1%**.
- Male employees have a higher attrition count than female employees.
- Employees aged **26–35 years** contribute the highest number of resignations.
- Employees from **Life Sciences** and **Medical** educational backgrounds account for the majority of attrition.
- Employees earning **Up to 5K** experience the highest turnover.
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives are the most affected job roles.
- Employee attrition is highest during the early years of employment.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Strengthen employee onboarding and engagement initiatives.
- Review compensation strategies for lower salary groups.
- Develop structured career progression opportunities for early-career employees.
- Conduct periodic employee satisfaction and engagement surveys.
- Prioritize retention initiatives for high-turnover departments.
- Enhance learning, development, and employee recognition programs.
